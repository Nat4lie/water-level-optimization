Mathematical Support

The water level threshold indicator

\begin{align*} HWL &= P_{25\%} WL,\\ LWL &= P_{75\%} WL,\\ MWL &= \frac{HWL + LWL}{2} \end{align*}

Utility function

\begin{align} U_i &= \mu_i + \epsilon_i,\\ \mu_i &= \alpha r_i + \beta m_i, \end{align}

Multinomial Logit model

\begin{equation} w_i = \frac{e^{\mu_i}}{\sum\limits_{j=1}^3 e^{\mu_j}}, \end{equation}

The optimal water level control algorithm

\begin{align}\label{objective} \max \quad &w_{i1} (x_i - HWL_i)^2 + w_{i2} (x_i - MWL_i)^2 + w_{i3} (x_i - LWL_i)^2\\ s.t. \quad & m_i \leq x_i \leq M_i,\\ & x_i \in \mathbb{R}. \end{align}
